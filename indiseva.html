What is Indi सेवा?

Indi सेवा is a one-stop platform that connects skilled and semi-skilled workers to customers in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities. Whether you need a carpenter, plumber, electrician, AC repairman, beauty service provider, or construction labor — we provide them at your doorstep within 10–20 minutes or at a scheduled time. Our aim is to solve unemployment, upskill workers, and bring instant services to underserved areas.

How It Works

1. Post Your Requirement

Users select a service, set a price, and choose “instant” or “scheduled” booking.

2. Smart Matching

Our system finds the nearest verified worker and confirms availability instantly.

3. Service Delivered

The worker reaches within minutes or at the scheduled time, with secure payment options.

Key Features

  • Instant 10–20 minute service
  • Dynamic pricing — user decides rates
  • Available in Tier-2/Tier-3 cities
  • Offline booking via missed call or WhatsApp
  • Worker verification and rating system
  • Skill development and certification for workers

Business Model

  • Marketplace model: Connecting service providers and customers.
  • Worker onboarding: Verified ID, skill check, and training.
  • Customer choice: View worker ratings and choose accordingly.
  • Service coverage: Home repairs, construction, personal care, and more.
  • Supply-demand based: Pricing and availability adjust to demand.

Revenue Streams

  • Commission per booking (percentage from worker or customer)
  • Subscription plans for frequent users
  • Premium profile boosts for workers
  • Ad space for local businesses
  • Partnerships with brands for tools and equipment
